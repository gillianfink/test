While New York City English is an integral part of the city’s identity, it is a dialect fraught with unfair class bias.

By means of familiarization we can unlearn the stigma often surrounding dialects that fall outside of the national “standard.”

Enter the site to discover the intricacies and charm of the New York Accent.
